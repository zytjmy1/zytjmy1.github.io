01 A direction

About me

A competition mindset, turning toward deeper questions.

I am a third-year Computer Science student at Nanjing Audit University. Competitions first taught me to enjoy the clarity of a hard problem: to model it, work patiently, and search for an elegant solution.

Now I am carrying that habit into research. I am learning to sit with questions before they have answers — especially in algorithms, theory, and the systems where intelligent agents meet. The destination is a life in research: graduate study, a PhD, and eventually academia.
